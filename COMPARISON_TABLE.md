## Detailed Comparison

### **NeatMail**
- **Best for:** Busy professionals who want instant results with zero setup
- **Key strength:** AI-powered labeling + drafts, works immediately after OAuth
- **Price:** Free (100 emails), then $9/mo
- **Setup time:** 2-3 minutes
- **Why choose NeatMail:** Fastest path to inbox zero. Works on Gmail AND Outlook. Free tier includes AI features.

---

### **Gmail Filters**
- **Best for:** Users who want to stay within Gmail
- **Key strength:** Built-in, no cost
- **Price:** Free
- **Setup time:** 15+ minutes per rule
- **Limitation:** Manual rules, no AI, time-consuming

---

### **Superhuman**
- **Best for:** Power users who live in email
- **Key strength:** Premium experience, native keyboard shortcuts
- **Price:** $99/month
- **Setup time:** 20+ minutes
- **Limitation:** Expensive, no free tier, limited Outlook support

---

### **HubSpot Sales Hub**
- **Best for:** Sales teams needing CRM + email
- **Key strength:** Integrated CRM functionality
- **Price:** Custom (min $50/month)
- **Setup time:** 1+ hours (requires CRM config)
- **Limitation:** Overkill for non-sales users, higher cost, complex setup

## Migration Path

If you're coming from:

**→ Gmail Filters:**  
Your Gmail labels work with NeatMail immediately. Enable NeatMail, disable old rules.

**→ Superhuman:**  
NeatMail offers the same AI drafting + labeling at 1/10th the cost. Your reading patterns are learned fresh (2-3 weeks).

**→ HubSpot:**  
If you don't need CRM, NeatMail handles inbox 10x faster. Use NeatMail for triage + Gmail/Outlook for execution.
